Comic Series: Mira And Missi

Description: This comic project is designed by me for my 'Robots and Creative Process' class where I tried to teach complex science topics in a playful way. I am creating this comic series that will focus on different complex topic for people with anxiety and learning disabilities.

The story evolves around a girl named Mira and her cat. She loves to study but her anxiety keeps her in the dark. Her cat, Missi, helps her to cope with anxiety and learning issues. Here is a glimpse of the beginning.
